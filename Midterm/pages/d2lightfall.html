Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is a popular action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It was released in September 2013 for PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360, and later in 2014 for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Microsoft Windows. The game is set in the fictional city of Los Santos, which is based on Los Angeles, and follows the story of three criminals: Michael De Santa, Franklin Clinton, and Trevor Philips. The game offers a massive open-world environment where players can explore the city and complete various missions and activities.

One of the key features of GTA V is its extensive single-player campaign, which allows players to switch between the three main characters at any time during the game. Each character has their unique abilities and personalities, and their stories intertwine throughout the game. Players can also engage in various activities such as heists, races, and sports, and customize their characters with different outfits and weapons. Additionally, the game features an online multiplayer mode, where players can team up with friends and complete missions or engage in various competitive activities such as races and deathmatches. GTA V has received critical acclaim for its storytelling, open-world design, and graphics, and is one of the best-selling video games of all time, with over 150 million copies sold worldwide.
