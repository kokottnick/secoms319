Video Game Catalog

This is a catalog of video games we are currently enjoying or looking foward too.
Each listing contains the cover art for the game and a breif description.

Assassins Creed Mirage is an upcoming game that hopes to take the Assassin's Creed saga back to its original roots. This will be set in the middle east where you will be completing assassinations for the secret creed of this title.

Singleplayer

Atomic Heart is an upcoming title where you are set in a distopian universe where robots and artifical intelligence have taken a strong root in society. However this AI has realized that they don't need human kind to survive. You will fight the AI and try to fight for humanity in this upcoming action title.

Singleplayer

Destiny 2 Lightfall is an upcoming expansion for the reknowned title Destiny 2, where you will be fighting the Witness in order to save humanity. The Witness has been teased for years and he is finally arriving with what is hoped to be the biggest expansion of the game yet.

Multiplayer

Sons of the Forest is an upcoming survival horror title that is a sequel to the well known game The Forest. Sons of the Forest hopes to expand on the original formula of the first game, by allowing a more dynamic crafting system, more exciting enemies, and a gripping story.

Singleplayer or coop

Star Wars: Jedi Survivor is an upcoming game where you are fighting back against the sith after Order 66. This game is a sequel taking place 5 years after the events of Star Wars: Jedi Fallen Order. Kal Kestus has expanded his Jedi skills in order to help the republic regain their claim over the solar system and fight back the sith.

Singleplayer

Wild Hearts is an upcoming monster hunter type of game that can be played in coop or singleplayer. This game hopes to expand upon the monster hunter formula by involving a more active open world, with a large variation in enemy types.

singleplayer and coop.
