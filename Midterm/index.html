Video Game Catalog

This is a catalog of video games we are currently enjoying or looking foward too.
Each listing contains the cover art for the game and a breif description.

Grand Theft Auto V is an action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. Players control three characters in a fictional city based on Los Angeles, committing crimes and completing missions while navigating the city's social and criminal underworld.

Campaign: 6 Hours

Red Dead Redemption 2 is a western-themed action-adventure game set in a vast open world where players take on the role of outlaw Arthur Morgan and explore the untamed frontier of the late 1800s. With rich storytelling, intense shootouts, and immersive environments, this critically acclaimed game offers a captivating journey into the heart of the American West.

Campaign: 7 Hours

Dirt Rally 2.0 is a rally racing simulation game that offers realistic and challenging gameplay, with a wide range of vehicles and tracks from around the world. Players can experience the thrill of high-speed off-road racing and hone their skills in this adrenaline-pumping game.

Multiplayer

Wolfenstein 2 is a first-person shooter game set in an alternate universe where the Nazi regime won World War II and control the world. The player takes on the role of BJ Blazkowicz as he sets out on a mission to take down the Nazi regime and bring freedom back to the world.

Campaign: 5 Hours

Fallout 4 is an action role-playing game set in a post-apocalyptic world where players must navigate through the ruins of Boston, Massachusetts as the sole survivor of Vault 111. The game allows players to make decisions and interact with the world, changing the outcome of the story and creating their own unique experience.

Campaign: 6 Hours

Half-Life: Alyx is a first-person shooter virtual reality game set in the Half-Life universe. Players take on the role of Alyx Vance as she fights against the alien combine in a post-apocalyptic world.

Campaign: 5 Hours

Hogwarts Legacy is an immersive, open-world action RPG. Now you can take control of the action and be at the center of your own adventure in the wizarding world.

Campaign: 7 Hours

L.A Noire is a neo-noir detective action-adventure video game set in 1947 Los Angeles, where players take on the role of LAPD detective Cole Phelps as he solves various cases and uncovers corruption within the department. The game features a unique blend of traditional gameplay, with a focus on interrogating suspects and solving puzzles, and stunning graphics that bring the city and its characters to life.

Campaign: 8 Hours

Just Cause 3 is an action-adventure video game developed by Avalanche Studios. It follows protagonist Rico Rodriguez as he attempts to overthrow the dictator of Medici, a fictional island nation in the Mediterranean, using a variety of weapons and gadgets.

Campaign: 7 Hours
